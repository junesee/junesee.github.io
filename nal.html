Neural Automated Labeling

Making AI training workflows usable, auditable, and scalable
Sector
Mobility / Autonomous Systems
What it is
An AI-driven data labeling platform supporting autonomous and advanced driver-assistance systems, designed to automate and manage large-scale training data workflows while maintaining traceability and quality.
Challenge
Manual and semi-automated labeling workflows were fragmented and difficult to audit, slowing iteration cycles and creating ambiguity across UX, AI, and engineering teams.
Role
UX Designer · Requirements Gathering · Workflow Design
What I did
  • Mapped end-to-end labeling and review workflows across data ingestion, automation, and validation stages
  • Gathered requirements from AI engineers, product owners, and operations teams to clarify system constraints and success criteria
  • Designed and prototyped automated labeling flows to make system state, progress, and confidence levels explicit
  • Used prototypes as alignment tools to bridge UX, AI logic, and engineering implementation decisions
Impact
  • Simplified labeling workflows, reducing manual steps by 30%
  • Improved auditability and confidence in labeling outcomes
  • Reduced ambiguity and rework between UX, AI, and engineering teams
