AI/ML Interface

Translating complex ML workflows into clear, human-centered interactions.
Sector
Manufacturing / Industrial AI
Overview
An ML platform for monitoring models and detecting anomalies in production systems.
Problem Context
While users were ML experts, the UI did not support their workflows efficiently.
Role & Scope
UX Designer · Requirements Gathering· Workflow Design
What I did
  • Worked with ML engineers and domain experts to map end-to-end workflows, dependencies, and decision points
  • Mapped expert decision paths, shortcuts, and critical moments in model and anomaly analysis
  • Redesigned UI flows to surface relevant signals faster and reduce unnecessary interaction steps
  • Collaborated closely with engineering to ensure designs reflected real ML constraints and data behavior
Outcome & Impact
  • Improved efficiency of expert workflows during monitoring and anomaly investigation
  • Reduced friction and interaction overhead in daily ML operations
  • Better alignment between expert mental models and system interactions
