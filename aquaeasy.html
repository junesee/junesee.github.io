AquaEasy — Smart Aquaculture UX

Improving aquaculture management with gamified AI
Sector
Aquaculture
What it is
A mobile platform for shrimp farm technicians that gamifies data entry and leverages AI to generate actionable insights for farm management.
Challenge
Farm technicians often skipped manual data entry, which reduced AI recommendation accuracy and limited actionable insights for pond management.
Role
UX Designer · End-to-End Flow Design · Mobile UX
What I did
  • Conducted user research with farmers to understand workflow pain points and motivations
  • Designed a gamified mobile interface to encourage consistent, accurate data entry
  • Prototyped interactive flows to validate usability, engagement, and data accuracy
Impact
  • Increased data entry by ~30%, improving the reliability of AI insights
  • Positive user feedback led to expanding the system to additional farm management tasks
  • Streamlined the daily workflow of farm technicians while maintaining high-quality AI-driven recommendations
