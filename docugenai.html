AI Document Search & Chatbot

Enabling fast and intuitive access to technical documents
Automotive· Enterprise AI · Conversational UX · Internal Knowledge Systems
Overview
Teams needed faster ways to find answers across large volumes of internal documents, manuals, and fragmented knowledge sources. Traditional search required users to know exact keywords, document names, or where information was stored — slowing productivity and increasing frustration. The opportunity was to improve knowledge discovery through conversational AI.
My Role
UX Designer responsible for experience design, conversation flows, prototyping, and usability validation in collaboration with product and engineering teams.
What I Solved
I designed a search and chat experience that allowed users to ask questions in natural language and receive relevant answers quickly, without needing to navigate multiple repositories.
Key experience principles included:
  • Simple conversational entry point
  • Clear answer summaries with source references
  • Guided follow-up questions to refine results
  • Reduced cognitive load compared with traditional search flows
  • Trust-building through transparent information sources
The goal was to make enterprise knowledge access faster, easier, and more intuitive.
Why This Decision Mattered
Many internal users do not search like experts. They search like people:
  • “How do I reset this?”
  • “Where is the latest process?”
  • What policy applies here?”
Designing for natural language behaviour reduced friction and lowered the learning curve.
Outcome
The solution improved speed and confidence in finding information, while reducing dependency on manual searching across multiple systems.
It demonstrated how conversational AI could increase productivity in document-heavy enterprise environments.
What This Demonstrates
  • Conversational AI UX
  • Enterprise search experience design
  • Knowledge management workflows
  • Trust and transparency in AI interfaces
  • Simplifying complex internal systemss

💭 Reflection
‍ Good AI search is not about chat for its own sake.
It is about helping people move from question to confidence with as little friction as possible..
